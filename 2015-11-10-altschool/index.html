Personalized, Whole-Child Learning

Our inspiring teachers draw on students’ passions, talents and learning styles to create an education that cultivates the whole child -- academically, socially, and emotionally.

Collaborative Community Involvement

Our teachers work with parents and partners in the community to offer a stimulating academic experience, taking advantage of the best people and places that the local area has to offer.

Continuous Research and Innovation

We use educational best practices and build innovative learning tools to continuously improve the AltSchool experience for students, parents, and teachers.
